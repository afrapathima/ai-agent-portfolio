# AI Customer Support Chatbot

## 1. Project Overview
The AI Customer Support Chatbot is a simple AI prototype designed to simulate an automated customer service system for an online mobile store. It can answer customer questions, solve common issues, and guide users through basic support processes.

This project demonstrates how AI can improve customer service efficiency by reducing response time and handling repetitive queries.

---

## 2. Key Features
- Answers customer queries about mobile products and services
- Provides order and delivery information responses
- Handles basic troubleshooting questions
- Simulates human-like customer support interaction

---

## 3. Sample Prompts and Responses

### Prompt 1:
User: My phone order is delayed. What should I do?

AI Response:
I understand your concern. Please check the following:
- Confirm your order tracking number
- Check delivery status on the courier website
- If delayed more than 3–5 days, contact support team

If needed, I can help you escalate the issue.

---

### Prompt 2:
User: Does your store offer warranty for phones?

AI Response:
Yes, all phones come with a manufacturer warranty.

- Standard warranty period: 6–12 months (brand dependent)
- Covers hardware issues only
- Physical damage is not included

---

### Prompt 3:
User: My phone is not turning on. What should I do?

AI Response:
Try these steps:
- Charge the phone for at least 20 minutes
- Try a different charging cable
- Perform a soft reset (hold power button 10–15 seconds)

If the issue continues, visit a service center.

---

### Prompt 4:
User: Can I return my phone after purchase?

AI Response:
Return policy depends on store terms:
- Returns allowed within 7–14 days
- Product must be unused and in original packaging
- Proof of purchase is required
