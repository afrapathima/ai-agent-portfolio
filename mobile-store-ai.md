# AI Mobile Store Assistant

## 1. Project Overview
The AI Mobile Store Assistant is a simple AI-based prototype designed to help customers choose mobile phones easily. It simulates a chatbot that can recommend phones based on budget, compare specifications, and answer basic product questions.

This project is inspired by a mobile shop management system and aims to improve customer decision-making using AI-style responses.

---

## 2. Key Features
- Recommends smartphones based on budget
- Compares phone specifications
- Answers customer queries about mobile phones
- Simulates a chatbot-style interaction

---

## 3. Sample Prompts and Responses

### Prompt 1:
User: I have 100,000 LKR budget. Which phone should I buy?

AI Response:
Based on your budget of 100,000 LKR, you can consider:
- Samsung Galaxy A series (A15 / A25)
- Redmi Note series (Note 13)
- Realme Narzo series

These phones offer good performance, battery life, and camera quality for the price range.

---

### Prompt 2:
User: Compare iPhone 11 and Samsung Galaxy A54

AI Response:
iPhone 11:
- Strong performance (A13 Bionic chip)
- Good video quality
- Limited battery life compared to newer models

Samsung Galaxy A54:
- Better battery life
- Higher refresh rate display (120Hz)
- More flexible Android system

Conclusion:
Choose iPhone 11 for performance and iOS ecosystem.
Choose A54 for battery and display.

---

### Prompt 3:
User: Which phone is best for gaming under 80,000 LKR?

AI Response:
Good gaming phones in this range:
- Xiaomi Redmi Note 13
- Infinix GT series
- Realme Narzo 60 series

Look for:
- At least 6GB RAM
- Strong processor (Snapdragon or Dimensity series)
